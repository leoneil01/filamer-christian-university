Frequently Asked Questions - Filamer Christian University

Admission

Question 1: What are the requirements for admission?

Answer: Applicants need to submit a completed application form, Live Birth Certificate (PSA), Report Card of previous school attended, Certificate of Good Moral(for transferee), Result of the Entrance Exam, 2 Pcs 2x2 Picture, Recommendation slip from FCU Guidance Office(for high school and college), White Long Folde, Report Card (Form 138 for college).

Academics

Question 1: What programs do you offer?

Answer: We offer a wide range of programs including Business Administration, Computer Science, Nursing, and Education.

Question 2: How do I register for classes?

Answer: Students can register for classes online through the university portal or by visiting the registrar's office.

Fees

Question 1: Are there any scholarship opportunities?

Answer: Yes, we offer various scholarships based on academic performance, financial need, and extracurricular involvement.

Campus Life

Question 1: What extracurricular activities are available?

Answer: We offer a variety of extracurricular activities including sports, clubs, student government, and volunteer opportunities.
