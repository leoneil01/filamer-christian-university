Why Study at Filamer?

AFFORDABLE AND REASONABLE COST OF EDUCATION

Tuition and fees are reasonable in relation to the income levels of most Capiznon households, and much more affordable than other private schools in key cities in the Visayas. Filamer offers various scholarship grants and financial assistance to deserving students.

GLOBALLY-COMPETITIVE EDUCATION

The Bureau of Immigration and Deportation granted Filamer the privilege of accepting foreign students and participates in multilateral and bilateral student exchange programs such as the University Mobility in Asia and the Pacific, and the Association of Christian Universities and Colleges in Asia and American Field Services.

FILAMER was also shortlisted as one of the Top 20 schools all over the Philippines to participate in the ASEAN International Mobility for Students.

PRESTIGE OF A UNIVERSITY EDUCATION

FILAMER is the first private university in Capiz. Most often, employers prefer job applicants with university degrees.

QUALITY AND EXCELLENCE

Filamer is the only school in Capiz awarded with Levels III and IV accreditation in most of its programs.

It is the only Higher Education Institution (HEI) in Capiz granted AUTONOMY and DEREGULATION by the Commission of Higher Education (CHED) and the biggest producer of board exam passers in Education, Criminology, Accountancy, Electronics, Engineering, and Nursing in Capiz.

Filamer is the only school in Capiz that produces Topnotchers in Education & Nursing board exams.
