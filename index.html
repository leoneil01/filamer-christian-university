Why Study at Filamer?
Filamer Christian University is the sole private university in Capiz distinguished with Level III to IV Accreditation across most of its programs, signifying rigorous quality assurance. It stands as the only Higher Education Institution (HEI) in Capiz granted Autonomy and Deregulated Status by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). Filamer consistently produces board exam passers in Education, Criminology, Accountancy, Electronics Engineering, and Nursing, including topnotchers in Education and Nursing board exams.
